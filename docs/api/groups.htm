--- layout: docs-api title: Groups ---

A group is a set of people. Every person can be either inside or outside the group. People in the group are also called group members, while people outside are called non-members.

type <group>Short information about a group
GET /db/{db}/groups/{id}/infoRead group meta-data
GET /db/{db}/groupsList all groups in the database
POST /db/{db}/groups/{id}/addAdd members to a group
GET /db/{db}/groups/{id}List all group members
POST /db/{db}/groups/{id}/removeRemove members from a group
POST /db/{db}/groupsCreate a group
DELETE /db/{db}/groups/{id}Delete a group
